# Casa de Historias

Casa de Historias is an Android mobile application dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich oral traditions, legends, and stories from the Sierra de Zongolica region in Mexico. The app serves as a digital repository where community members can record, share, and discover bilingual stories in both Spanish and Náhuatl.

## Mission

Our mission is to:

- **Preserve Cultural Heritage**: Safeguard the oral traditions of indigenous communities before they are lost
- **Promote Bilingualism**: Celebrate both Spanish and Náhuatl languages equally
- **Connect Communities**: Enable storytellers and listeners to share cultural knowledge across the Sierra de Zongolica region
- **Educate Future Generations**: Provide an accessible platform for younger generations to learn their ancestral language and stories

## Technology Stack

Casa de Historias is built with modern Android development tools:

- **Jetpack Compose**: Modern declarative UI framework for native Android
- **Kotlin**: First-class language for Android development
- **Firebase**: Cloud storage for stories, authentication, and media hosting
  - Firestore for story data
  - Firebase Authentication for user management
  - Firebase Storage for images and audio files
- **Room**: Local database for offline story access and favorites
- **MapLibre**: Open-source mapping for geographic story visualization
- **Media3 (ExoPlayer)**: Audio playback for oral narrations
- **Hilt**: Dependency injection for clean architecture

## Who Is This For?

<Note>
Casa de Historias is designed for community members, cultural preservationists, educators, and anyone interested in the rich oral traditions of Sierra de Zongolica.

Whether you're a storyteller wanting to share your knowledge, a student learning Náhuatl, or a researcher documenting indigenous cultures, Casa de Historias provides the tools to connect with these living traditions.
