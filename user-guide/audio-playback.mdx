## Overview

Casa de Historias uses **Media3** (formerly ExoPlayer) to provide high-quality audio playback for story narrations. Audio brings legends to life with authentic voices from the Sierra de Zongolica.

<Note>
Not all stories have audio. Look for the **play button icon** on story cards to identify stories with audio narration.
</Note>

## Finding Stories with Audio

### In the Story List

Stories with audio display a circular play button icon in the bottom right of the card:

```kotlin
if (!story.audioUrl.isNullOrBlank()) {
    Box(
        modifier = Modifier
            .size(48.dp)
            .clip(CircleShape)
            .background(Brush.radialGradient(
                colors = listOf(AguaSagrada, AguaSagradaOscura)
            )),
        contentAlignment = Alignment.Center
    ) {
        Icon(
            imageVector = Icons.Default.PlayArrow,
            contentDescription = "Tiene audio",
            tint = NocheProfunda
        )
    }
}
```

### In the Discover Tab

The Discover screen has a dedicated **"Con Audio Original"** section showing stories with audio narration:

- Compact cards display story title and narrator
- Each card includes a play button overlay
- Tap any card to open the story detail and start playback

<Tip>
Use the Discover tab's "Con Audio Original" section to quickly find all stories with audio narration.
</Tip>

## Playing Audio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a story with audio">
    Tap any story card that displays the play button icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Locate the audio player">
    On the story detail screen, the audio player appears near the top or bottom of the content.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Control playback">
    Use the player controls:
    - **Play/Pause**: Start or stop audio playback
    - **Seek bar**: Drag to jump to a specific timestamp
    - **Current time**: See elapsed time and total duration
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Media3 Integration

Casa de Historias uses **Media3** (AndroidX Media3) for audio playback:

```kotlin
// Dependencies in build.gradle.kts
implementation(libs.media3.exoplayer)      // Core ExoPlayer
implementation(libs.media3.ui)             // UI components
implementation(libs.media3.session)        // Media session support
```

### Why Media3?

- **High compatibility**: Supports MP3, M4A, WAV, and more
- **Streaming**: Plays audio directly from Firebase Storage URLs without downloading
- **Buffering**: Handles network conditions gracefully
- **Background playback**: Continue listening while browsing stories
- **Low latency**: Starts playback quickly

## Audio Storage

Audio files are stored in Firebase Storage:

```
gs://your-app.appspot.com/audio/{storyId}/narration.mp3
```

Each story's audio is stored with:
- **Unique ID**: Prevents filename conflicts
- **Public URL**: Accessible via HTTPS
- **Security rules**: Read access for authenticated users

<Warning>
Audio files are streamed, not downloaded. Ensure you have a stable internet connection for uninterrupted playback.
</Warning>

## Audio Formats

### Supported Formats

- **MP3**: Most common, excellent compression (recommended)
- **M4A**: AAC-encoded, good quality
- **WAV**: Uncompressed, high quality but large file size
- **OGG**: Open format with good compression

### Recommended Settings

For uploading audio:
- **Format**: MP3
- **Bitrate**: 128 kbps (good balance of quality and file size)
- **Sample rate**: 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- **Channels**: Mono (sufficient for voice) or Stereo
- **Maximum duration**: 10 minutes (for user experience)
- **Maximum file size**: 50 MB

<Tip>
Use mono recording for voice narration. It reduces file size by 50% with no quality loss for spoken content.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Audio won't play">
  - Check your internet connection
  - Ensure the story has an `audioUrl` (not all stories have audio)
  - Verify Firebase Storage security rules allow read access
  - Try closing and reopening the story
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Audio keeps buffering">
  - You may have a slow or unstable internet connection
  - Wait for the audio to fully buffer before playing
  - Consider using WiFi instead of mobile data
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Audio stops when I navigate away">
  Background playback may not be fully implemented yet. Keep the story detail screen open while listening.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I download audio for offline listening?">
  Offline audio downloads are not currently available but may be added in a future update.
</Accordion>

## Best Practices

### For Listeners

- Use headphones for the best audio quality
- Listen in a quiet environment to appreciate the narration
- Follow along with the written text to learn Nahuatl pronunciation
- Share stories with audio to spread oral traditions

### For Contributors

- Record in a quiet space to minimize background noise
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
- Use a good quality microphone if possible
- Keep narrations under 10 minutes for best user experience
- Compress audio to MP3 at 128kbps before uploading

<Tip>
Audio narration is the heart of oral storytelling. When creating stories, consider adding audio to preserve the authentic voice and emotion of the narrator.
</Tip>
