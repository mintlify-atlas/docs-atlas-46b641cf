## Preserving Cultural Heritage Through Storytelling

Casa de Historias is a mobile application dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich oral traditions of the Sierra de Zongolica region. The app provides a platform for communities to record, share, and discover legends in both Spanish and Nahuatl, ensuring these stories are preserved for future generations.

Casa de Historias is built with Jetpack Compose, Firebase, and modern Android development practices to provide a seamless, offline-capable experience for preserving cultural heritage.
